Yuhang Guo (郭宇航)

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at UNSW Sydney. Before that, I obtained my master's and bachelor's degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). My current research is mainly about computational economics: algorithmic game theory, computational social choice, etc.
